# Code of Conduct

This project aims to be rigorous, calm, and respectful.

## Expected behavior

- critique ideas and artifacts without personal attacks
- keep claims precise and sourced
- assume good faith while staying honest about errors and limitations
- avoid harassment, discrimination, or intimidation

## Unacceptable behavior

- abusive or demeaning language
- harassment based on identity or background
- doxxing, threats, or unwanted contact
- knowingly misrepresenting another person's work or evidence

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact <mailto:jackgaff@umich.edu>. Reports will be handled discreetly.
